About US:
We are a Saudi brand and we have extensive experience in of offering gelato in the traditional Italian way, and it is available for individuals and companies for various occasions, weddings, and events. We offer different sizes and types of carts according to your preferences.

Owner Message:
The Ice Cream Village is the beginning of a story of passion to taste all that is distinctive in the world of ice cream and share it with its fans, big and small... We dream of diversity and innovation
Our capabilities:
Catering to all occasions and parties with a full crew, both men and women, with carts loaded with all kinds of delicious and healthy ice cream, at all times
Certificates and experience:
They hold Italian certificates in the field of ice cream and natural gluten, with many years of experience in the hands of experienced Saudis
